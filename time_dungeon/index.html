The Future's Past dungeon is modeled to serve as a vault for
event gear awarded in legacy event cycles and no longer
obtainable any other way on outwar

new world area

future's past dungeons are accessible with a time shard which can be obtained two different ways:

During War of Zhul & Trial of Power events

As players collect and turn in Summoning Shards, they will receive a Time Shard at different milestones when they are awarded other items on the existing quest line:

  • 25 Summoning Shards = Player receives Potent Time Shard

  • 250 Summoning Shards = Player recieves Cerebral Time Shard

  • 1000 Summoning Shards = Player receives All Time Shard

Talk to Future's Past in room #8

3 quests to exchange Quest Shards for Time Shards:

  • Give 2 Quest Shards (100 points) for 1 Potent Time Shard

  • Give 20 Quest Shards (1000 points) for 1 Cerebral Time Shard

  • Give 40 Quest Shards (2000 points) for 1 All Time Shard

Potent Time Quest Line

Talk to Traveler's Complex Refinery in room #4

QUEST STEP

REWARD: Refined Potent Time Shard

All Time Quest Line
