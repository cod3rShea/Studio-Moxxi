The Elemental Aborition's Dungeon is a complex concept designed
to present players with new choice in gaming strategy.
Players can enter the dungeon and remain there for as long as they want to,
but will be unable to re-enter after exiting without first paying the enterence fee.

Limited Access Areas

Red (Key Area 1) is only accessable to players carrying the Ignited Fuser item

Green (Key Area 2) is only accessable to players carrying the Cursed Signet item

Players start by completing the Ignite the Fuser quest given by the Elemental Aborition. They receive an ignited fuser (quest item, player bound - expires in 120 minutes).

The Ignited Fuser allows the player access to the Red Key Area 1 which is where they will find the Spiritual Aborition in room #2.


Talk to Spiritual Aborition who offers 1 quest:

The Cursed Signet (repeatable)
Exchange 1 Ignited Fuser for 1 Cursed Signet (quest item, player bound)

The Cursed Signet is a quest item that does not expire. It will grant players access to Green Key Area 2. This mechanic allows the player to stay in the green area for as long as they want to, but if they leave, they will not be able to return through the red area without first obtaining a new Ignited Fuser (paying more elemental fusers). This creates a virtual wall and forces players to make a prioritization choice of when to enter and how long to stay in the dungeon.

After obtaining the Cursed Signet, players can roam freely in the dungeon for as long as they would like to. The dungeon contains 6 total raids:
